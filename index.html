Sandy Bay Holiday Park - is a quiet, shady and grassed holiday park

Ideally located in Busselton, Western Australia on the foreshore of the pristine calm waters of Geographe Bay and only 6km west of Busselton town centre, Busselton Jetty, Train and Underwater Observatory.

Geographe Bay is a calm, sheltered area ideal for families, young and old, with superb swimming, fishing, snorkeling, crabbing and beach combing all year round.
